Calm robotics is a philosophy and a set of principles for robots that can be simple, faithful, sustainable companions over time. The goal is to start at the bottom and build up, creating multi-layered robots with a focus on ambient, low-expectations companionship. These robots aren't intended to be family members, playmates, or educational toys. They mostly do their own thing, responding to their environment with an observable inner life that takes on primary importance in their design.  R Read more about this.
